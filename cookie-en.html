How cookies are used on this portfolio.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that the browser may store in order to ensure correct functionality, technical preferences and coherent navigation.

Cookies used

  • technical cookies required for the banner to work and for storing the selected preference
  • no profiling or marketing cookies are directly set by this portfolio

Consent management

The cookie banner allows the user to accept or reject the preference storage. The choice is stored locally in the browser so the banner is not shown on every visit.

How to disable cookies

The user can intervene at any time through the browser settings by deleting or blocking the cookies already stored on the device.
